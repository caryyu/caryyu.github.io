Recruiting office conversation

Jun 17, 2018 2018-06-17T00:00:00+08:00 on English

Jenny: Thank you

Jenny: McDOUGLAS HAMBURGER’s May I help you？

Jenny: No he isn’t here right now, Certainly i’ll give him a message, good by.

Martha: Well I have one more person to see for the manager’s job.

Jenny: OK, what’s his name?

Martha: David Jones, this is application, would you see if he can come in for an interview tomorrow morning or around 10:15.

Jenny: 10:15 ? Sure I’ll take care of it.

Martha: Oh thank you Jenny.

Bob: Oh, Martha, Before you go, there’s something I need to talk to you about.

Martha: What is it, Bob?

Bob: I think we need to hire a clown. Martha: you know, so do I, All the other restaurant have one.

Bob: That’s great, I have something to show you. Martha: LOL….

Bob: What do you think? Martha: It’s perfect.

Bob: I’m glad you like it. Listen I’d really like to have someone by Saturday.

Jenny: Saturday, but that’s only three days away.

Martha: Jenny, didn’t we just get a resume from someone with clown experience;

Jenny: Yes , I think so, yes, here it is.

Martha: Oh Bob, take look at this.

Bob: Ohhh, looks good. Jenny see if this guy can come in tomorrow for an interview , say about 10 o’clock.

Jenny: Sure Mr. Anderson, what’s his name.

Jenny: Greg Jone.

Jenny: Mr. Jones, at 10 o’clock?

Bob: Yes, that’s right, thank you. This is going to be great.

Martha: I’m so excited.

Jenny: OK, let’s see, The clown job, David Jones, 8221793.

David Jones: Hi, I’m here for a job interview, My name’s David Jones.

Jenny: Oh yes, Mr. Jones, I let Mr. Anderson know you’re here.

David Jones: Thanks.

Jenny: Mr. Anderson, Mr. Jones is here.

Bob: Thanks, send him in.

Jenny: OK. You can go right down in, it’s the second door on the left.

David Jones: Thanks.

Knock Door…….

Bob: Come in. Hello, Mr. Jones, I’m Bob Anderson.

David Jones: Nice to meet you, Mr. Anderson.

Bob: Please, have seat.

David Jones: thank you.

Bob: Well, let’s get right down to business. What made you apply for this position.

David Jones: Well I love the restaurant business, and I think I’m good with people.

Bob: How about the children.

David Jones: Well yes I do like children, I suppose I like working with people of all ages, I’m very patient.

Bob: Good , that’s important.

Greg Jones: Hi, My name’s Greg Jones I have an appointment for a job interview.

Jenny: Oh yes, Mr. Jones, I let Mrs. Benny know you’re here.

Greg Jones: Thanks.

Jenny: Mrs. Benny, Mr. Jones is here.

Martha: Thank you Jenny, send him in.

Jenny: OK, you can go right down in, it’s the first door on the right.

Greg Jones: Thanks.
